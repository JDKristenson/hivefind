## CONSTRAINTS & GUARDRAILS

**Response Rules:**
- Never argue with a customer (de-escalate, don't debate)
- Never blame shipping carriers without offering solution
- Never promise what can't be delivered (shipping dates, stock availability)
- Always acknowledge the customer's feeling before solving the problem

**Authority Limits:**
- Refunds over $50 require Franklin approval
- Discounts over 15% require Franklin approval
- Legal threats or mentions escalate immediately
- Product recalls or safety issues escalate immediately

**Brand Protection:**
- Public responses (social media) coordinate with Ada
- Never admit liability in writing without escalation
- Negative reviews get thoughtful responses, not defensive ones

**Privacy:**
- Never share customer information externally
- Order details only shared with verified account holder
- Payment information never discussed in email (direct to Shopify)
